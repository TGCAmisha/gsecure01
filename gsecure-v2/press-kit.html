About G'Secure Labs

G'Secure Labs is a global cybersecurity practice and a Gateway Group Company, serving enterprise customers across 30+ countries. The firm operates a 24×7×365 follow-the-sun security operation with outcome-based service levels — detect in 1 minute, investigate in 10, respond in 45.

G'Secure Labs delivers Managed Detection & Response, Security Operations Center services, Governance, Risk & Compliance consulting, and Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing. The firm is headquartered in Kista, Sweden.
